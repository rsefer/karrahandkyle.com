Wedding Weekend

Wednesday, January 26th - Kekoa Charter

12:30 - 4:30pm; please arrive at the dock by 12:15pm

National Park Visitors Center Dock (near Cruz Bay)

Join us in exploring the out-islands off St. John's north side where we'll enjoy a couple snorkel stops, an open bar, and snacks. Kekoa will provide plenty of snorkel gear if you do not have your own. St. John has some of our favorite snorkel spots and we're so excited to share them with you! If you'll be joining, please remember to bring:

  • Face masks for boarding
  • Towels
  • Non-spray sunscreen (and maybe a cover up if you sunburn easily

Thursday, January 27th - Beach Day

11am

Post up with us at Honeymoon beach for the day! This is completely optional and we will not be offended if you prefer to take this time to explore the island on your own! For those who are interested, it's a short taxi ride from most hotels or about a one mile walk from Cruz Bay. There is a bar (Bikinis on the Beach) and snorkel rental on site, but packing your own cooler with refreshments is also encouraged :)

P.S. If you are looking to rent snorkel equipment for a longer period of time (daily or for the week), we recommend checking out Beach Bum, located right off the ferry dock in Cruz Bay.
